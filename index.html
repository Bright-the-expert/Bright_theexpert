Who I am

The developer behind BTE

I'm Bright, an e-commerce web developer working under the name Bright_theexpert (BTE), with over 7 years of experience in e-commerce. I specialize in building and customizing online stores, primarily on Shopify, with additional experience across other e-commerce platforms, for businesses that need a store which is both visually polished and fully operational from day one.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), and I've completed formal training through the Shopify Academy as well as Google's Digital Marketing & E-Commerce program, to deepen my technical and marketing grounding around the platform.

My work centers on the fundamentals that determine whether a store actually performs: structured store setup, precise theme customization, reliable checkout flows, and fast, mobile first load times, since that's where most shoppers engage.

This site is actively maintained, with project case studies added as they're completed, rather than populated with placeholder work.

Questions, answered

FAQ

Who do you work with?
Anyone looking to launch or improve an e-commerce store, new business owners setting up their first store, and existing stores that need a redesign, speed fixes, or a platform move.
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope, a basic store setup can take a few days, while a full custom build or migration takes longer. You'll get a clear timeline as part of the quote before work starts.
Do you offer support after the store goes live?
Yes, I check in after launch to make sure everything's working correctly, and I'm available for fixes or adjustments afterward.
What do you need from me to get started?
A rough idea of what you're selling, any branding you already have (logo, colors), and access to your store or hosting if one already exists. If you're starting from scratch, I can help you set that up too.
How do payments work?
Pricing is agreed upfront based on the scope of the project. Reach out with details about what you need and I'll send a quote.
Can you work with a store I already have, or do I need to start over?
I can work with an existing store, redesigning it, fixing issues, or optimizing it, without needing to rebuild from scratch, unless a full rebuild genuinely makes more sense for your case.
What if I'm not sure exactly what I need yet?
That's fine, that's what the discovery call is for. Reach out with a rough idea of what you're trying to do, and we can figure out the right scope together.
What platforms do you work with besides Shopify?
Primarily Shopify, with additional experience on WooCommerce and Wix depending on what best fits your project.
Do you provide an agreement before starting work?
Yes, once scope and pricing are agreed, you'll get a clear written summary of what's included before any work begins.
What if I need changes after the project is marked complete?
Small fixes shortly after handover are covered. Larger changes or new features are treated as a separate, quoted piece of work.

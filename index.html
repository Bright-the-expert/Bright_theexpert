Who I am

The developer behind BTE

I'm Bright, an E-Commerce web developer working under the name Bright_theexpert (BTE), with over 7 years of experience in E-Commerce. I specialize in building and customizing online stores, primarily on Shopify, with additional experience across other E-Commerce platforms, for businesses that need a store which is both visually polished and fully operational from day one.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), and I've completed formal training through the Shopify Academy as well as Google's Digital Marketing & E-Commerce program, to deepen my technical and marketing grounding around the platform. I recently joined Zenthrop Agency, working alongside a wider team on E-Commerce projects.

My work centers on the fundamentals that determine whether a store actually performs: structured store setup, precise theme customization, reliable checkout flows, and fast, mobile first load times, since that's where most shoppers engage.

This site is actively maintained, with project case studies added as they're completed, rather than populated with placeholder work.

What I do

Services

🛒 01

Store Setup & Launch

Full store setup from scratch, domain connection, theme install, products, collections, and payments configured and ready to sell.
This includes setting up your store's structure from the ground up: navigation menus, collections, product upload, shipping zones, and payment providers, so nothing is left half-configured when you go live.
🎨 02

Theme Design & Customization

Adjusting and building out themes so your store matches your brand instead of looking like a default template.
Rather than using a theme exactly as downloaded, sections get adjusted, colors and fonts get aligned with your brand, and layout quirks get fixed so the store feels intentional, not generic.
⚡ 03

Store Speed Optimization

Cleaning up bloated code, oversized images, and slow apps so your store loads fast on every device.
This covers compressing images, removing unused app scripts, and cleaning up theme code, since slow load times are one of the biggest reasons visitors leave before buying.
🔄 04

Platform Migration

Moving a store between Shopify, WooCommerce, and Wix carefully, keeping products and data intact.
Product data, customer records, and order history are mapped and moved carefully between platforms, with testing before and after to make sure nothing gets lost in the process.
🔌 05

App Integration

Setting up reviews, email marketing, and other store apps so they work together instead of against each other.
Apps get chosen and configured with your specific store in mind, rather than installing everything available, so your store doesn't end up slow and cluttered with tools you don't need.
📱 06

Mobile Optimization

Making sure the store looks and works properly on phones, not just on a desktop preview.
Every layout gets checked specifically on phone screens, buttons, menus, and checkout, since most traffic to a typical store comes from mobile, not desktop.
🔍 07

SEO Optimization

Structuring product pages, collections, and metadata so your store is actually discoverable in search results.
Product titles, descriptions, image alt text, and URL structure get reviewed and cleaned up so search engines can actually understand and rank your pages.
📦 08

Inventory & POS Integration

Connecting your online store with physical retail and inventory systems so stock stays accurate across every channel.
Stock levels get synced between your online store and physical point-of-sale system, so you're not manually updating numbers in two places and risking overselling.
📈 09

Conversion Rate Optimization

Refining product pages, cart flow, and checkout so more visitors actually complete a purchase.
This looks at where visitors drop off, cluttered product pages, confusing checkout steps, and adjusts them based on what typically improves completed purchases.
🛠️ 10

Ongoing Maintenance & Support

Keeping your store updated, fixing issues, and handling small changes after launch so it keeps running smoothly.
This covers routine checks, applying updates, fixing small bugs, and handling minor content changes so the store keeps running smoothly long after launch.
🔒 11

Payment Gateway Setup

Configuring secure, reliable payment options so customers can check out with the methods they trust.
Payment providers get configured and tested end-to-end, including currency settings and fraud protection basics, so transactions go through reliably from day one.
📊 12

Analytics & Tracking Setup

Setting up analytics and conversion tracking so you can actually see what's working and what isn't.
Tools like Shopify Analytics or Google Analytics get properly connected and configured with the right goals, so the numbers you see actually reflect what matters for your store.
🌐 13

Domain & Hosting Setup

Connecting your custom domain and getting hosting configured correctly so your store is properly live.
This includes connecting your domain registrar to your store, setting up DNS records correctly, and making sure SSL/security certificates are active.
🔐 14

Store Security Hardening

Tightening up store access, permissions, and settings to reduce the risk of unauthorized changes or breaches.
Staff access levels, app permissions, and account settings get reviewed and tightened, reducing the chances of unauthorized changes or data exposure.
🧾 15

Tax & Shipping Configuration

Setting up accurate tax rules and shipping rates so orders are charged and fulfilled correctly.
Tax rules get set up according to where you legally need to charge them, and shipping rates get configured to match your actual carrier costs, not guesswork.
💬 16

Live Chat Integration

Adding live chat or chatbot tools so customers can get quick answers while browsing your store.
A chat widget gets added and configured with sensible default responses, so customers browsing your store can get quick answers instead of abandoning their visit.
🌍 17

Multi-Currency & International Setup

Configuring your store to sell properly across different countries and currencies.
Currency conversion, region-specific pricing, and international shipping settings get configured so customers outside your home country have a smooth experience too.
🖼️ 18

Product Photography Editing

Cleaning up and formatting product images so they look consistent and professional across your store.
Background cleanup, consistent cropping, and color correction get applied across your product images so your catalog looks unified rather than mismatched.
📧 19

Email Marketing Setup

Setting up abandoned cart emails, order updates, and basic email flows to keep customers engaged.
Automated flows like abandoned cart reminders and order confirmations get built out, so follow-up with customers happens without you manually sending every email.
🔗 20

Third-Party API Integration

Connecting your store with external tools and services your business already relies on.
Whether it's a CRM, an inventory system, or another business tool, integrations get set up and tested so data flows correctly between your store and the tools you already use.

Questions, answered

FAQ

Who do you work with?
Anyone looking to launch or improve an E-Commerce store, new business owners setting up their first store, and existing stores that need a redesign, speed fixes, or a platform move.
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope, a basic store setup can take a few days, while a full custom build or migration takes longer. You'll get a clear timeline as part of the quote before work starts.
Do you offer support after the store goes live?
Yes, I check in after launch to make sure everything's working correctly, and I'm available for fixes or adjustments afterward.
What do you need from me to get started?
A rough idea of what you're selling, any branding you already have (logo, colors), and access to your store or hosting if one already exists. If you're starting from scratch, I can help you set that up too.
How do payments work?
Pricing is agreed upfront based on the scope of the project. Reach out with details about what you need and I'll send a quote.
Can you work with a store I already have, or do I need to start over?
I can work with an existing store, redesigning it, fixing issues, or optimizing it, without needing to rebuild from scratch, unless a full rebuild genuinely makes more sense for your case.
What if I'm not sure exactly what I need yet?
That's fine, that's what the discovery call is for. Reach out with a rough idea of what you're trying to do, and we can figure out the right scope together.
What platforms do you work with besides Shopify?
Primarily Shopify, with additional experience on WooCommerce and Wix depending on what best fits your project.
Do you provide an agreement before starting work?
Yes, once scope and pricing are agreed, you'll get a clear written summary of what's included before any work begins.
What if I need changes after the project is marked complete?
Small fixes shortly after handover are covered. Larger changes or new features are treated as a separate, quoted piece of work.

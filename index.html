Who I am

The developer behind BTE

I'm Bright, an e-commerce web developer working under the name Bright_theexpert (BTE). I specialize in building and customizing online stores — primarily on Shopify, with additional experience across other e-commerce platforms — for businesses that need a store which is both visually polished and fully operational from day one.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), and I've completed formal training through the Shopify Academy to deepen my technical grounding in the platform.

My work centers on the fundamentals that determine whether a store actually performs: structured store setup, precise theme customization, reliable checkout flows, and fast, mobile-first load times — since that's where most shoppers engage.

This site is actively maintained, with project case studies added as they're completed, rather than populated with placeholder work.
